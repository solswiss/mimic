  • <mark></mark> used, in general, to display the colors of items
  • <details>[hidden stuff] ... <summary>[toggle title]</summary></details> for the vendors - clean, right?

  • If you're wondering how this displays HTML as plaintext, the trick is to replace angle brackets with their corresponding HTML entities; &lt; (also &#60;) is the less than symbol and &gt; (also &#62;) is the greater than symbol. Wanna know more? Use the inspect tool!
