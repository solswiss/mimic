Machine Closed Inventory

Cobra weave. Easy weave for beginners; this was among the first I learned. If you want to try learning paracord, weaves, or knots, get 325 (thin) and 550 (med.) paracord, a lighter, scissors or a blade. Optional quality of life items include buckle, jig, and lacing needle. I don't care, so my weaves are closed with a loop and makeshift knot or diamond knot.

Glass pebbles and lantern. Not sure why I did but putting colorful things that pass light on a light source makes for quite a nice centerpiece.

Meiji Almond Chocolate. It's only natural to compartmentalize things, so naturally I store things in a very organized manner. What's this have to do with the item? Everything. Unlabeled storage is terrible. The solution could be to label things, or just use pre-existing pre-labeled containers like a lazy mimic. Truth be told I really like this method, but its application is limited by the containers you already have, so I can only explain so much!

Curtains. Put curtains everywhere! For doorways, see noren or bead curtains. Closets are better with curtains. Some advice: floor-to-ceiling curtains in white make a room seem taller!
